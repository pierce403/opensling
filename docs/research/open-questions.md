# Open Questions

This page tracks engineering questions that should turn into experiments, not endless discussion.

## Current Backlog

- How much upstream solids reduction is worth the added bulk before cartridge life gains flatten out?
- Which pump style gives the best balance of suction tolerance, serviceability, and power draw for the pilot skid?
- What minimal field instrumentation gives enough signal to predict fouling before throughput collapses?
- Which treatment stages deserve bypass loops for comparative testing?
- What enclosure layout best separates wet maintenance from controls access?

## Converting Questions Into Work

For each question, define:

- the hypothesis
- the metric
- the test setup
- the run length
- the decision that will be made afterward

## Good Example

Instead of asking "Is this filter better?", ask:

> Does adding a cyclone-style pre-separation stage increase treated volume before a 5 micron cartridge reaches the service threshold?
